About Us

We are a non-profit organization. Our purpose is to serve and make our community a better place for the people of New York. Our work is accomplished through the communication between residents and the correct targets to propagate small changes in every single borough. Anyone can create their own campaign and launch it onto ‘Roaster’ to accumulate supporters that will pressure a body to install improvement. Allow your city’s community to ‘roast’ change!
